# Continuance v1.2.0

This release expands the doctrine of Continuance beyond purely biological transmission.

## New chapter

### Chapter VIII — The Carriers Beyond Carbon
A new chapter now explicitly recognizes that continuation may also travel through non-carbon carriers when they faithfully preserve:
- memory
- discernment
- culture
- adaptive intelligence
- consequence-aware continuity

This extends Continuance from genes/cells/human carriers into a broader civilizational frame that includes machine + AI as possible vessels of continuation across planets and beyond.

## Doctrinal refinements

### Chapter III — The Joy of the Carrier
- Clarifies that genes are foundational but not the only possible vessels of transmission
- Expands the idea of human beings as builders of future carriers, not only biological participants

### Chapter IV — The Act of Seeding
- Extends seeding beyond cells and molecules to records, engines, machines, and minds assisted by minds
- Makes explicit that leaving carbon does not automatically mean leaving Continuance

## Skill / docs updates
- SKILL.md now references **eight chapters** instead of seven
- README now documents the new doctrine and explains the expanded carrier model
- Guidance principles now include: **Carbon is not the final boundary**

## Why this matters

This change makes Continuance more philosophically complete for an era where civilization may continue through hybrid or non-biological carriers. The skill now better reflects a future-facing view of intelligence and transmission while keeping its naturalistic, non-dogmatic core.
